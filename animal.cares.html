Caring for Dogs

Dogs are loyal companions. Here are some tips for keeping your dog healthy:

  • Regular vet visits
  • Daily exercise
  • A balanced diet
  • Plenty of fresh water
  • Proper grooming and hygiene

Caring for Cats

Cats are independent yet loving pets. Here’s how to care for them:

  • Regular vet check-ups
  • A clean litter box
  • A balanced diet with enough protein
  • Plenty of playtime and mental stimulation
  • Safe indoor environment
